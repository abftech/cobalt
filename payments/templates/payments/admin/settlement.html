System Settlement

This screen can be used for settlement with clubs. If the "Generate Xero Invoices" option is selected then an invoice will be created in Xero for the payments. If there are errors when creating the Xero invoice then payments are not deducted from the account and the settlement for that organisation will not be processed. Other settlements will still continue.

Clubs with balances below their minimum balance are not shown here. You can view them from Organisations with balances
